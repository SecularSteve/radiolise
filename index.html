Settings

Here you may customize to your needs.

Theme:
A simple and minimalistic theme. A simple and minimalistic dark theme. A vivid and colorful theme. A simple yet elegant theme. A simple yet elegant dark theme.
Color mode:
Set whether a random or a custom theme color should be used.
Theme hue: COMING SOON
Pseudo-visualization
Displays an audio visualization based on random values during a running audio stream. A wide desktop viewport is required.
Relax mode
When the specified time has passed, hides the entire content except the name of the current station, increases its font size and temporarily enables ‘pseudo-visualization’.
Relax timeout (sec):
Set the period of time until ‘relax mode’ is triggered.
Load icons
Defines whether official station icons should be loaded. If disabled, simple CSS-based placeholders are shown instead.
Transitional effects
Enables simple and elegant transitional effects. You should disable this option if your device does not have enough resources.
